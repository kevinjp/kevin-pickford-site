      Ghost Rain:Sunstone City: one the largest and wealthiest cities in the United States of America. but this skyscraper filled sprawling metropolis is also one of the most crime filled and hosts some of the most powerful criminals in Sunstone. Detective Sammy Rain, a hero cop in Sunstone, is in command of a team from some interesting and maybe even unlikely allies to remove crime from her city. but Sammy also has her demons and past to face up to.






      Fractuired Reich:The year: 1942, Nazi Germany is on the brink of winning world war 2, the allied powers are also on the brink of surrendering, and it is all thanks to one man, a high ranking Nazi commander who betrayed his country and used everything he knew about the allies to win the war for the axis powers. The allied powers send two of their very best generals and their men to destroy their Nazi commanders men and bring him to justice, maybe even turn the tide and win the war.






      Shattered Salvation:1932: America is in the tight grip of the great depression. However, crime rules over the country unchallenged. No law, no police force, not even the government can even come close to regaining power or a sense of justice. Enforcer, hitman and mercenary Michael Salvatore, is ruthless and is the best in the country. until he discovers his target: the woman he is sent to kill. once he discovers this woman is his target, he begins his path to redemption for himself and tries to gain hope for this woman's future.
